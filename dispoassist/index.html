The Problem

Patients ready for discharge, stuck waiting for coordination

Every hospital faces the same challenge: patients medically ready for discharge, but coordination takes hours. Case managers and nurses spend their day on phone tag — calling SNFs, arranging transport, tracking family members, chasing authorizations. This procedural work pulls clinical staff away from patient care.

The Solution

DispoAssist handles everything between discharge decision and patient exit

Remote expeditors execute the repetitive phone calls, follow-ups, and documentation — freeing your nurses and case managers to focus on patient care and complex cases.
