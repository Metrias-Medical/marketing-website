Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Metrias Medical, Inc. ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at metriasmedical.com (the "Site") and interact with our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing and using the Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways depending on how you interact with our Site and services.

Website Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device when you visit the Site, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This information is collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies.

Contact Form Information

If you submit a contact form on our Site, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, phone number, and message content. We use this information to respond to your inquiry and to communicate with you about our services.

LinkedIn API Data

We use the LinkedIn API to manage our company page. Through this integration, we may collect and process data related to our LinkedIn organization profile, including engagement metrics, follower information, and content interaction data. This data helps us understand audience engagement and optimize our company page content.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Site, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our services. These may include session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device).

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Site and services
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you marketing and promotional communications (with your consent where required)
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends to enhance user experience
  • Monitor and analyze Site performance and functionality
  • Manage our LinkedIn company page and optimize our social media presence
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements

LinkedIn Data

Metrias Medical uses the LinkedIn API to manage and optimize our organization's LinkedIn company page. Here's what you should know about our LinkedIn data practices:

  • Data Accessed: We access aggregated engagement data, page analytics, follower demographics, and content performance metrics through the LinkedIn API.
  • Data Usage: We use this data solely to understand our audience, measure campaign performance, optimize our company page content, and improve our social media strategy. We do not use this data for direct personal marketing to individual LinkedIn users.
  • Data Storage: LinkedIn data is processed and stored securely in accordance with this Privacy Policy and LinkedIn's terms of service. We retain this data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share LinkedIn-sourced data with third parties, except as required by law or in connection with our authorized business partners who assist us in managing our LinkedIn presence under strict confidentiality obligations.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including web hosting providers (Vercel/GitHub Pages), analytics services (Google Analytics), and error tracking services (Sentry). These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: If Metrias Medical is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include secure socket layer (SSL) encryption, secure data storage, and limited access to personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Healthcare Data Disclaimer

Metrias Medical works with hospitals to provide remote discharge coordination services. However, this website and the information collected through it do not directly collect, store, or process Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA. While our expeditors may work within hospital systems to facilitate discharge coordination, any interaction with patient clinical information is governed by separate Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) between Metrias Medical and our hospital partners, which implement comprehensive HIPAA-compliant safeguards.

If you have questions about how your health information is handled by our hospital partners, please contact your hospital directly.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal requirements and exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Cookie Preferences: You may manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on the Site.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@metriasmedical.com.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.
